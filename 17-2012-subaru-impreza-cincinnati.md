## Verdict

**This is the sleeper of the search.** Three responsible owners, 100% Subaru dealer service for 13 years, the FB20's two biggest failure modes (oil consumption + head gaskets) addressed at dealer level, manual transmission eliminates CVT risk entirely, and the current engine only has ~50k miles.

The two minor damage events are cosmetic non-issues. The clutch disc at 100k+ is the main unknown — budget $1,200 if it needs doing.

At $4,900 from the same reputable dealer as the Cincinnati 335i, this is arguably the **safest mechanical buy in the search**. The BMW is more fun; the Subaru is more bulletproof. **Ranked #2.**
