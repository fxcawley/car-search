# Used Car Search — Overview & Rankings

## Background

This document consolidates a multi-day used car search conducted in late March 2026. The buyer is shopping for a reliable daily driver in the **$3,000–$6,000** price range, primarily looking at listings in **Michigan, Ohio, Indiana, and Illinois**. Each vehicle was evaluated by pulling its Carfax report, analyzing the ownership chain, identifying known mechanical issues for that platform, and assessing the risk-to-value ratio. The buyer prioritized:

- **Clean service history** (ideally dealer-documented)
- **No or minimal accident history**
- **Mechanically simple / proven drivetrains**
- **Low total cost of ownership** (parts + labor)
- **Rust belt survivability** (all candidates are Midwest cars)

Sixteen vehicles were evaluated in total. Individual reports are in separate files within this directory.
