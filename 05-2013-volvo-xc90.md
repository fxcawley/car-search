## Known XC90 3.2 Issues

- **Transmission (Aisin TF-80SC):** The #1 weak point — valve body issues, shudder, harsh shifting
- **Oil trap / PCV system:** Causes oil consumption and leaks if neglected
- **Front suspension:** Control arm bushings, struts
- **AWD angle gear seal:** Known leak point, can starve the angle gear

## The Service Gap — How Bad Is It?

The 3.2L inline-6 (B6324S) is Volvo's naturally aspirated six — one of their most durable engines. It's not fragile like a turbo. That said, ~96k miles with no records is a lot even for a tough engine.

What likely happened: Owner 3 in Detroit either got oil changes at quick-lube places that don't report to Carfax, did DIY oil changes, or actually neglected it.
