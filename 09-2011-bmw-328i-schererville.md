## Negotiation

$4,800 at a small dealer is negotiable. **Offer $4,000–$4,200 cash**, citing the unknown status of water pump, oil pan gasket, and suspension at 148k. That puts you in the same price range as the Ann Arbor car but with a significantly better-preserved vehicle.

## Verdict

**This is the #1 pick.** Best history, fewest miles of any BMW evaluated, zero accidents, all-dealer service, valve cover already done. The N52 inline-6 is one of the most reliable BMW engines ever made. At a negotiated price of ~$4,200, this is the strongest value in the entire search.
