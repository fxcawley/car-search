## Verdict

**Do not buy this car.** The moderate-to-severe all-body damage, repo, odometer exemptions, N54 complexity, and dealer carousel make this the second-worst Carfax in the entire search (after the Audi A4). The extra 70 hp over the 328i is not worth the risk.
