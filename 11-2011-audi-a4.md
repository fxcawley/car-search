## The Smoking Gun: Oil Consumption Test at 7,960 Miles

The very first thing Audi Middleburg Heights did for Owner 1 was an oil consumption test under 8,000 miles. This means the original owner walked into the dealer complaining the brand-new car was burning oil. This isn't a generic service item — it's a formal diagnostic triggered by a customer complaint.

This car has the **EA888 Gen 1 oil consumption defect.** The 2009–2012 Audi 2.0T had defective piston rings that caused excessive oil burning — so widespread there was a class-action lawsuit and Audi extended the warranty to 80k miles. **No record that the pistons/rings were ever replaced.** This engine has been burning oil for its entire life.
